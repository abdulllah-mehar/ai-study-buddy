🚀 AI Study Buddy

An AI-powered study assistant designed to help students prepare for exams like the SAT through interactive tutoring, step-by-step guidance, and personalized learning.

🎯 Problem

Students often rely on passive learning methods or generic answer systems that don’t actually teach them how to think.

Most tools:

Give direct answers ❌
Don’t track weaknesses ❌
Lack real tutoring experience ❌
💡 Solution

AI Study Buddy acts like a real tutor, not just a chatbot.

It:

Guides students step-by-step
Encourages critical thinking
Tracks performance and weak areas
🔥 Key Features
🤖 AI Tutor Mode (Core Feature)
Does NOT give direct answers immediately
Asks guiding questions
Helps students solve problems step-by-step
📊 Weakness Tracker
Tracks correct/incorrect answers
Identifies weak topics (e.g., Algebra, Grammar)
Helps students focus on improvement
🧠 Smart Explanations
Clear, structured solutions
SAT-style explanations
Follow-up questions for deeper understanding
